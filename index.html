OUR STORY

About Platform 909

Platform 909 is a specialty coffee shop in Chino Hills serving handcrafted coffee, premium matcha, fresh breakfast, and artisan sandwiches. From signature lattes to house-made paninis, every item is prepared with quality ingredients in a welcoming, modern café atmosphere.

Whether you're stopping in for your morning coffee or meeting friends over lunch, Platform 909 offers a menu that blends creative drinks with fresh, satisfying food.
